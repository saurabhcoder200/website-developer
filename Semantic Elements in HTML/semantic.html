Facts about instagram

Instagram, owned by Meta, is a social media platform focused on photo and video sharing. It boasts over 2 billion monthly active users and is popular for its visual focus, Stories feature, and Reels. Instagram is also a significant platform for businesses and influencers. Here are some key facts about Instagram: Basic Information:

Founded: October 6, 2010. Acquired by: Facebook (now Meta) in 2012 for $1 billion. Headquarters: Menlo Park, California. Current Head: Adam Mosseri. Focus: Photo and video sharing.
