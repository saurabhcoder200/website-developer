Facts about instagram

Instagram, owned by Meta, is a social media platform focused on photo and video sharing. It boasts over 2 billion monthly active users and is popular for its visual focus, Stories feature, and Reels. Instagram is also a significant platform for businesses and influencers.

On the daily, business accounts are keeping things active, posting an average of 1.55 times on their main feed. It seems like Instagram is a hotspot for brand engagement, with 62.4% of its users either following or checking out brands and products on the platform. And here’s a fun fact – a whopping 90% of users are following at least one business.
