Cricket: The Gentleman's Game

Cricket is more than just a sport; it is a passion for millions around the world. From Test matches to T20 thrillers, the game unites fans across continents.

The origins of cricket date back to the 16th century in England. Over time, it evolved into an international sport with formats like Test cricket, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20. Today, cricket is celebrated worldwide, especially in countries like India, Australia, and England.

"You don’t win or lose the games because of the 11 you select. You win or lose with what those 11 do on the field." — Rahul Dravid

Legendary Players

  • Sachin Tendulkar — Known as the 'God of Cricket', he has over 34,000 international runs.
  • Virat Kohli — One of the modern-day greats, famous for his consistency in all formats.
  • MS Dhoni — Captain Cool, led India to multiple ICC trophies.
